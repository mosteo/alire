# Platform Maintainers

Not to be confused with maintainers of the Alire project itself, platform
maintainers have the responsibility to oversee that `alr` works as intended in
a particular supported platform.

Responsibilities chiefly are:

- Housekeeping of system crates as new releases of underlying packages occur.
- Check the build reports of crates on their platform and address any detected
  issues, by fixing or pinging the actual crate maintainers.
- Initially:
   - Report or fix issues with the build of `alr` on that platform.
   - Add the platform as one of our CI configurations.

The Alire developers mentor with the tasks related to the initial setup.
